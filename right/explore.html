NISSAN Formula E

Nissan joined Formula E as the first Japanese manufacturer, focusing on transferring EV technology from track to road. They compete with innovative powertrains, aiming for wins while showcasing their electric performance.
NISSAN Super GT

Nissan boasts a rich history in Super GT (formerly JGTC), primarily competing with their GT-R in the top GT500 class. They are known for fierce rivalries and have secured numerous championships, showcasing their high-performance engineering.
NISSAN GTR LM NISMO

The Nissan GT-R LM Nismo was a highly unconventional front-engine, front-wheel-drive LMP1 prototype designed for the 2015 Le Mans 24 Hours. Despite its bold concept, it ultimately struggled with performance and reliability, leading to the project's quick cancellation.
